## Footer — Design brief
Dark surface #111111, border-t white/10. 4-col grid: brand block (logo + mono label + body + orange phone CTA) + 3 link columns (Services, Navigation, Contact). Legal row at bottom with border-t.

## Composition
Column headers in mono 11px white/30 uppercase. Links in font-sans 500 white/60 hover orange. Year dynamic via new Date().getFullYear().
