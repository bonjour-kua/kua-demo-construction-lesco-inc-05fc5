## About — Design brief
Light surface #FAFAF8. 12-col grid: 5 cols photo (4:5 portrait), 7 cols text. Orange accent block floating bottom-right of photo. Eyebrow + H2 + body + bullet list + inline text CTA.

## Composition
Photo has subtle hover scale. Text side: eyebrow in mono, H2 uppercase black with orange accent word, two body paragraphs, 4-item orange-dot list, arrow text link.
