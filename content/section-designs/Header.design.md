## Header — Design brief
Sticky dark surface (#1B1B1B), full width, h-20 lg:h-24. Logo left (image + text lockup). Nav center-right with 5 links. Phone CTA button (orange accent) rightmost. Mobile: hamburger + icon phone button.

## Composition
