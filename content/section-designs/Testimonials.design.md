## Testimonials — Design brief
Light surface #FAFAF8. 12-col split: 4 cols left (label + H2 + name nav list + dot indicators), 8 cols right (giant blockquote with orange highlight word). Auto-rotating every 5s. Click name to jump.

## Composition
Quote uses getHighlightedQuote to orange-color one phrase. Opening quote mark decorative in orange/20 opacity. Names as buttons with left border accent.
